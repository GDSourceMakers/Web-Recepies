3-Ingredient Hazelnut Brownies
Description
Simple to do and the brownies come out perfect! Add some chocolate chips on top for extra yum.
Ingredients
  • 13 oz (370 g) chocolate hazelnut spread
  • 2 piece eggs
  • ½ cup (65 g) flour
Add all to Shopping list
  1. Preheat oven to 350ºF (175ºC) .
  2. In a large bowl, combine ingredients.
  3. Transfer mix to square baking dish .
  4. Bake for 15 minutes .
  5. Allow to cool for 5 minutes and then carefully cut into squares.
  6. Enjoy!
